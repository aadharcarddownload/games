Doodle Cricket

Google launched Doodle Cricket in 2017 in honour of a significant cricket event. In this undemanding and exciting game, which has little resemblance to real cricket, the objective is to score as many runs as possible as a batter. You just need to set your clicker to swing the bat, but the trick is not to get out when all you need is to count. It also has other target areas such as targeted body shots or goal-scoring rates. That is just for fun and must be good for any cricket lover or anyone who wants to spend several hours playing games. Give Doodle Cricket a try!
